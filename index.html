About Us

The company "Decomatic" was founded in 1997. Initially established as a manufacturing firm specializing in window design, the organization has gradually expanded its focus beyond interior spaces. The production of decorative panels has become another promising direction for our company. Each year, we strive to increase sales volumes through competitive pricing and the expertise of our highly qualified personnel, who work in the office, the store, and the production facility. Regular additional training is provided to enhance the quality of our services. The equipment in our production facility is constantly modernized, positively impacting the production timelines. Decorative panels are made of a solid and durable composite material obtained through extrusion with the application of hot stamping on the surface to create a marble texture. This material is a PVC composite, and the surface of the profile is further reinforced with a co-extrusion layer. PVC panels with a "marble" finish are a prospective decorative finishing material for various interiors, residential, and public buildings. They offer the aesthetics of marble along with lightweight properties and a lower cost. Key advantages of the material include environmental friendliness, corrosion resistance, affordability compared to marble, ease of processing and installation, the ability to shape the profile in any form, and durability on curved surfaces. The material is not susceptible to the effects of moist interior conditions. Size: 2440 mm x 1220 mm.
